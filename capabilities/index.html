AGI&M ASSETS INC.

Capabilities

AGI&M ASSETS INC. provides hardware-focused capabilities across energy and power infrastructure, structured for program-based delivery on infrastructure-grade projects.

Energy Storage Systems

Commercial and industrial BESS hardware for grid-tied and off-grid deployments. Our scope covers system-level hardware, integration readiness, and deployment alignment. Products are certified to UL9540, IEEE 1547, and applicable NEC standards.

Power & Grid Infrastructure

Electrical distribution hardware and balance-of-system components for regulated utility and EPC environments. We support safe and reliable interconnection across a range of project scales.

Data Center Power Interconnect

Rack power interconnect hardware for hyperscale and AI data center environments. Selected for density, long-cycle reliability, and compatibility with mission-critical uptime requirements.

EMS & Control Systems

Energy Management System software and hardware for microgrid and BESS deployments. Our platform supports grid-tied, off-grid, and hybrid operating modes with full SCADA integration.
