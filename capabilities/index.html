AGI&M ASSETS INC.

Capabilities

AGI&M ASSETS INC. is a Canadian private holding company. All capabilities are delivered through operating subsidiaries and long-term supply programs in energy infrastructure, power equipment, and autonomous systems.

Active programs with SDG&E, PepsiCo, Black & Veatch, Shell, Blink Charging, ERCOT, PJM, and Hydro-Québec. 500+ MW deployed across North America since 2019.

Energy Infrastructure — Energize Solutions Inc.

Our primary US operating subsidiary (Texas) delivers EMS platforms, BESS hardware, and power infrastructure for utilities, EPCs, and industrial operators. The EMS platform supports 15 strategy modules with C1–C7 hardware interlock chains, Modbus TCP, DNP3, and IEC 61850 communication protocols. Active programs comply with SDG&E interconnection, CA Rule 21, and IEEE 1547-2018.

BESS hardware supply uses CATL and EVE cells, with UL9540, UL9540A, and NFPA 855 certification coverage. Compatible PCS vendors include Power Electronics (Spain), SMA, and Sungrow. All deployments include engineered submittals and commissioned FAT protocols.

Power Equipment Supply

Long-term supply framework programs for EHV, HV, MV, and LV power transformers under ANSI/IEEE C57 standards. Engineered submittal packages, certified factory acceptance testing (FAT), and schedule-committed delivery for North American EPC and utility clients. Fiber optic supply covers OSP outdoor cable, campus fiber, and FTTX passive products with OTDR-traceable documentation.

Data Center Power Infrastructure

Power interconnect hardware and infrastructure supply for hyperscale and AI data center operators. Focused on system density, long-cycle reliability, and mission-critical uptime across NA markets.

Autonomous Systems — ARCBOS

Operating subsidiary developing the SnowBot autonomous commercial snow removal robot for the North American market. Targeting the US$22.5B North American snow removal industry. Pre-A stage ($2M USD). RaaS pricing model: $120–200K per unit per year. Full investor materials available at ref.arcbos.com.
