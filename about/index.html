AGI&M ASSETS INC.

About

AGI&M ASSETS INC. is a Canadian holding and operating company delivering energy and power infrastructure hardware for North American markets.

Corporate Structure

We operate through a three-entity structure spanning Canada and the United States. AGI&M Holdings Inc. and AGI&M Assets Inc. are incorporated in Canada. Energize Solutions Inc. is incorporated in Texas and serves as the primary US operating entity.

How We Work

Our delivery model is program-based. We work directly with EPCs, system integrators, and infrastructure developers to supply certified hardware, support integration, and maintain long-term operational alignment.

Headquarters

We are headquartered in Canada with active programs across North America.
