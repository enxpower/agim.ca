AGI&M ASSETS INC.

About

AGI&M ASSETS INC. is a Canadian private holding company with operating subsidiaries in energy infrastructure and autonomous systems across North America.

Corporate Structure

AGI&M ASSETS INC. is incorporated in Canada and serves as the parent holding entity. Operating subsidiaries include Energize Solutions Inc. (Texas, USA) and ARCBOS.

How We Work

Our delivery model is program-based. We work directly with EPCs, utilities, system integrators, and infrastructure developers to supply certified hardware, support integration, and maintain long-term operational alignment.
